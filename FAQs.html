Q1: Is Pepsi a carbonated beverage?
Yes, Pepsi is a carbonated soft drink.
Q2: What is the difference between Pepsi and Coca-Cola?
The main difference lies in the flavor and recipe. While both are carbonated soft drinks, Pepsi has a slightly sweeter and citrusy taste compared to Coca-Cola.
Q3: Does Pepsi contain caffeine?
Yes, Pepsi contains caffeine. The amount of caffeine may vary depending on the specific product and serving size.
Q4: Is Pepsi suitable for vegetarians or vegans ?
Yes, Pepsi is suitable for both vegetarians and vegans. It does not contain any animal-derived ingredients.
Q5: How many calories are there in a can of Pepsi?
The number of calories in a can of Pepsi can vary depending on the serving size. Generally, a standard 12-ounce can of Pepsi contains around 150 calories.
